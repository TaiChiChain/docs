---
title: "Committee"
---

Governance work is mainly carried out by the governance committee. Initially, the governance committee is a member of several institutions configured by default, which will be written in the initial block. Subsequent changes to the governance committee will be submitted by members of the governance committee to the election of committee members. Committee members vote, and after the vote is passed, a new governance committee will be formed.

The Governance Committee is mainly responsible for chain-related governance work, mainly including:

1. Nodes join and exit, vote on the proposals for joining and exiting nodes, the proposals will be accepted if the votes exceed a certain number of votes, and the nodes can join the blockchain network or exit the network;

2. Node upgrade, vote on the upgrade proposal, after the vote is passed, all the nodes of the chain will be upgraded to the specified version;

2. Election of members of the Governance Committee to elect the new members of the Governance Committee;

3. Economic-related adjustments, including gas fee adjustments, reward and punishment mechanism changes, etc.
