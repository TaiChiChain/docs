## Intro to HKDC

`HKDC` is the native coin pegged 1:1 to the Hong Kong Dollar. Users interact with `Axiom` may need to pay in HKDC.

Users can visit [here]() to buy `HKDC`. In testnet, you can visit [faucet]() to get some test coin to interact with the blockchain system.
